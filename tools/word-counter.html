How to Use the Word Counter Tool

  1. Enter or paste your text in the text area above.
  2. The tool will automatically count the words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs.
  3. View the results in the statistics section.
  4. Click the "Copy Text" button to copy your text to clipboard.
  5. Click the "Clear" button to reset the text area and results.

You can use this tool to check the word count for essays, articles, blog posts, assignments, or any other written content.
